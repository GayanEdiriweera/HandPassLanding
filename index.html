Hand Pass

Hand Pass is an easy way to share information in person. The next time you want to give your phone number, email address or web link to someone in person, try using Hand Pass.

Hand Pass lets you make QR Codes from your information. It does not send your information over a network connection. It simply represents your information as a QR Code.
